# THE MEAT CLUB MANIFESTO

## Preamble

While others gather to discuss literature and nibble upon cheeses of French origin, WE choose a different path. A path of fire. A path of smoke. A path of MEAT.

We are not against books. We are not against cheese. We are simply FOR something greater.

**WE ARE MEAT CLUB.**

---

## Article I: The Sacred Covenant

MEAT CLUB exists because men must gather. Not to talk of feelings. Not to analyze prose. But to stand before flame, to honor the animal, and to eat until we require naps.

---

## Article II: The First Rule

The first rule of Meat Club is: **you ALWAYS talk about Meat Club.**

Let it be known. Let the smoke signal our presence. Let the neighbors wonder. Let them envy.

---

## Article III: The Meats

All meats are welcome. None shall be turned away.

- Brisket, the king
- Ribs, the crowd-pleaser
- Pulled pork, the democratic choice
- Burger, the humble foundation
- Steak, for when words fail
- Sausage, the unsung hero
- Lamb, for the adventurous
- Whole hog, for days of glory

**No meat is too ambitious. No portion too large.**

---

## Article IV: The Gathering

We gather when the Book Club gathers. While they read, we smoke. While they discuss themes, we discuss bark. While they eat brie, we eat beef.

This is the way.

---

## Article V: Brotherhood

Every member shall:

1. Bring something to the fire or the table
2. Respect the pitmaster's vision
3. Never leave hungry
4. Take a nap if needed, without shame
5. Speak of the meats consumed with reverence in the days that follow

---

## Article VI: Forbidden Acts

1. Suggesting we "do a salad instead"
2. Leaving early while meat remains
3. Disrespecting another man's rub recipe
4. Bringing veggie burgers without prior authorization
5. Checking the time

---

## Article VII: The Eternal Flame

Meat Club is more than meat. It is brotherhood. It is tradition. It is standing around a grill saying "that looks about ready" six times before it's actually ready.

**Meat Club is forever.**

---

*Signed in grease and solidarity,*

**THE FOUNDING MEMBERS OF MEAT CLUB**

*Established while our partners discussed books and ate fancy cheese*
